The Best Movies According to Nithya

My top 3 movies of all time

Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani

This is a Bollywood movie which talks about an introvert girl who explores herself when she runs into a Manali trip with a group of complete strangers.
It focuses on how important it is to shape one's life according to his/her own wish.

Saptha Sagaradaache Ello

This is a Sandalwood movie starring Rakshit Shetty and Rukmini Vasanth where both of them have beautifully depicted the emotional intensity of love between a man and a women.

Seetha Raamam

This is a Tollywood movie that takes you all the way back to the era of post cards picturising an eternal love story between a soldier and a princess.
